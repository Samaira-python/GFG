About Gear for Good

We aim to collect Medical Equipments Donations upon partnering up with hospitals around the city, who then distribute the same to those in need.




About The Founder & Venture

Hi! I'm Samaira Malik, a student and environmental activist based in Bangalore. I love everything basketball related, reading and playing the piano :)

A little bit about why I started this venture:

I realised the need of medical equipments first hand thanks to the aftermath of covid. Due to the shortage of medical equipments, my family was struggling to find even the basic ones, which made me wonder the struggle the lesser privledged go through on a daily basis.

After researching for a bit, i figured out a hosital in my city that collects equipments and donates them to those in need who visit their hospital. So, i partnered with them and broadcasted a message about their foundation accross the city, initiated the awarness drive and managed the logistics of the collection. This was a huge success- I got the response of over 150 donors in a span of 2 nights.

In the future, I want to partner with hospitals across different cities so more people can get benefited- which was the main purpose of Gear For Good to begin with!

How this works:

1. Select a hospital you want to donate to and fill the form link. 2. Communicate with the hospital upon filling the form to discuss further details.
3. Donate your equipments & help save lives!
